ADMISSION RULES

    The academic year is from March to February . Admission starts from 2nd Week of November every year. Prospectus and admission form can be obtained from the office on prescribed payment.

    A child who attained at least 4 years and 6 Months of age will be eligible for admission test in class prep. The Principal reserves the right of accepting or rejecting any child for admission. A child who is not mentally fit or suffer from a disease that may adversely affects others will NOT BE ADMITTED.

    Parents are required to fill prescribed registration form available from school office. Every student seeking admission will have to appear for Written Test / or interview as given here under; PLEASE NOTE THAT THE REGISTRATION DOES NOT IN ANY WAY GUARANTEE ADMISSION

   The children who have registered will have to appear for an admission test, which may be written or oral as laid down by the School for different levels. The test will be held for English, Hindi, Mathematics and/or any other subject which may be considered necessary.

   The selection of the candidates will depend upon the child's performance in the test/interview and availability of seats in the concerned class.

GENERAL RULES

   The selection of the candidates will depend upon the child's performance in the test/ interview and availability of seats in the concerned class.

   The date of the result of the interview & the last date for the submission of fee will be specified at the time of the test/interview.

   Based on the admission test and other criteria, the School reserves the right to admit any student who is found fit for admission or refuse admission without assigning any reason.

   Admission - Any false or incorrect information furnished may risk selection and enrolment.

   The names of the children who are selected as per the admission procedure will be displayed on the notice board.
