GD Goenka School Patna

It has an imposing and architecturally well designed building with vast play grounds and attractive infrastructural facilities to cater physical, intellectual, emotional and aesthetic growth of the students through creative and innovative programmes. The school campus spreads over twelve and a half acres of land, overlooking green fields and beautiful mountains. The school opened its doors in April 2008 to 399 students from the Upper KG to class six. Within a span of few years it expanded to the senior wing, the admin block and state of the art Science Labs, Audio Visual Room & Amphitheater.

Mission
